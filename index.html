Bio

Jake Ball was born in 1997 in Washington DC. He soon moved to New Hampshire where he grew up. After graduating highschool, he spent a year at Elon University before transferring back to UNH. He graduated with a degree in Psychology and a minor in Philosophy. He then moved to Boston where he currently works for a property management firm, while taking Masters classes on the side.
